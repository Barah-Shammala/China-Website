HISTORY SNIPPET

Numero eSIM as an international mobile app by a Spanish based company, Smarteletec, and was launched in globally on Google Play and Apple Store in 2018. It started as a virtual numbers app with voice services at low rates.

In 2019, it was one of the first telecom services to jump on the eSIM trend, and the only virtual numbers app that provides eSIM mobile data plans, making it a complete telecom solution that fully replicates a mobile network operator.
